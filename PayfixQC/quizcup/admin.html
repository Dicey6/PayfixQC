💸 PAYMENT WORKFLOW

Payments are sent via Jupiter Mobile. No private key is stored on any server.

  1. Approve a submission in the Submissions tab
  2. Click 💸 Pay via Jupiter — the modal shows the wallet & amount
  3. Approve the transaction in the Jupiter mobile app
  4. Payment is recorded automatically once confirmed on-chain

If any submissions show stuck in processing (from the old auto-pay system), click ↺ Reset to Approved and then use the modal to pay them.
